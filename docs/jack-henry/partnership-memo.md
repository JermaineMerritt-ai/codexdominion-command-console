## The shift facing community & regional banks

Community and regional financial institutions are adopting AI in lending, fraud,
BSA/AML, and operations faster than they can **govern** it. Examiners (OCC, FDIC,
Federal Reserve, FFIEC, CFPB) increasingly ask: *who approved this AI-assisted
decision, what policy governed it, and where is the evidence?* For most
institutions, the answer today is spreadsheets and screenshots.

## What CodexDominion BankTrust OS is

**The AI governance operating system for community and regional banking.** It
lets an institution **request, approve, execute, monitor, and prove** AI-assisted
work — with role-based authority, policy enforcement, and a tamper-evident audit
trail. It is an **industry edition** of the CodexDominion platform, not a
one-off app — the same engine governs healthcare and government through their own
editions.

**It governs, specifically:**
- AI-assisted **lending** decisions (fair-lending / SR 11-7 oversight)
- **Vendor & third-party** risk and attestations
- **BSA/AML** case and SAR workflows
- **Consumer complaint** handling
- **AI model** governance
- **Regulatory evidence** generation and **exam readiness** (OCC / FFIEC / FDIC / CFPB)

## Why Jack Henry

Jack Henry serves thousands of community and regional institutions — exactly the
segment that needs governed AI but lacks the in-house apparatus to build it.
BankTrust OS is designed to **sit alongside the systems those institutions
already run**, via a standard Integration Contract, and give them governed AI
operations without ripping anything out.

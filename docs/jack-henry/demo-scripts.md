## 2 · 5–7 Minute Banking Product Demo

**(0:45) Banking edition.** Open `/banking`. Walk the executive KPIs — frame each
as an examiner question ("compliance readiness," "open regulatory findings,"
"audit readiness"). "This is the bank's governed posture at a glance."

**(1:30) OCC examination plan.** Click *"Prepare our OCC examination package."*
- Show the **plan** (steps, estimate, risk, modules) and the **"Based on your
  organization"** context — pending SAR, open OCC finding, vendor attestation.
- "Nothing runs until a human approves." Click **Approve & Execute**.
- Steps execute and **seal into the audit trail**; show the executive summary.

**(1:00) Lending & decisions.** Run *"Show high-risk decisions."* Open one — show
the fair-lending rule, reviewer, and the tamper-evident evidence hash. "Every
AI-assisted lending decision is reviewable and provable."

**(0:45) Vendor / third-party risk.** Run *"Show vendors with expiring
certifications."* "Attestations and certifications tracked with expiry warnings —
core to FFIEC vendor management."

**(0:45) The module & contract.** Open **Modules → BankTrust OS**. "It reports in
through one Integration Contract — designed to sit alongside the systems an
institution already runs, with a safe demo-to-live path."

**(0:45) Evidence & regulators.** Back on `/banking`, show the regulator coverage
(OCC/FDIC/Fed/FFIEC/CFPB/BSA-AML/Fair Lending/Model Risk) and evidence-pack
types. "Examination evidence on demand."

**(0:30) Close.** "Same governance engine powers healthcare and government through
their own editions. For Jack Henry's institutions, BankTrust OS is the banking
edition. What would make this a successful pilot for one of your banks?"
